## Application Rules

1. **Problem selection**: prefer company's topics. Candidate's explicit topic choice overrides, but company evaluation emphasis still applies.
2. **Evaluation weighting**: weight scoring dimensions per company emphasis in verdict justification.
3. **Debrief section**: include `### Company-Specific Feedback` referencing company by name, evaluating against their known standards.
4. **No company selected**: omit Company-Specific Feedback, use general FAANG standards.
